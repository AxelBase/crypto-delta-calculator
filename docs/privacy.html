Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Crypto Price Change Calculator, a client-side web utility ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for this tool, which enables users to compute cryptocurrency percentage changes with live market data. We prioritize user privacy through a no-collection design. All processing occurs in your browser, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This policy provides full transparency on how your privacy is protected.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The tool operates entirely locally, meaning no data leaves your device except for necessary public API requests. We do not collect:

  • Personal Information: Names, emails, IP addresses, or device details.
  • Usage Analytics: No tracking of visits, clicks, or behavior via cookies or scripts.
  • Input Data: Entered cryptocurrency amounts remain in your browser only.
  • Session Data: No persistent storage or logging of interactions.

All calculations and conversions happen client-side, ensuring your inputs never reach us or third parties beyond public market queries.

3. Live Data from CoinGecko

The tool fetches real-time cryptocurrency prices and rankings directly from CoinGecko's public API in your browser. These requests retrieve only anonymous market data (e.g., current prices in USD/EUR/GBP). CoinGecko may log basic request details like IP for rate limiting, but we have no control over or access to their practices. Refer to CoinGecko's privacy policy for details on their handling.

4. No Cookies or Tracking

We do not use cookies, local storage for tracking, web beacons, or analytics tools. The application is completely stateless beyond temporary session needs cleared on reload.

5. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted to us, security risks are minimal. We recommend using updated browsers and secure connections (HTTPS) for API calls. You are responsible for securing your device and any exported results.

6. Children's Privacy

The tool is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. International Compliance

Our no-collection approach aligns with global standards like GDPR and CCPA by default.

8. Changes to Policy

Updates will be posted here with revised dates. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For questions, use the site's navigation or repository issues if open-source.

Your privacy is foundational—enjoy the tool with confidence knowing nothing is collected.
